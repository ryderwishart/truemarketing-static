Can't I rely on advertising?

Spending money on advertising can have an immediate ROI, but you will be locked into a dependence on paid ads to drive traffic.


Because when the ads stop, the traffic stops.


Forget the fact that when most people see an ad, their reflex is to ignore it.


Ad campaigns can be an effective way of jumpstarting a marketing campaign to begin driving traffic to your website, but ads are only one small part of an evergreen marketing campaign (one that offers a long-term, repeating ROI).

Can't I just hire an employee to market for me?
